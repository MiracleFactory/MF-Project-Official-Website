Project and Passion Driven Development

Here at Miracle Factory, we are pushing the idea of project driven development. Every project is the result of a passion and an idea. Apart from following a simple Code of Conduct, the community has no restriction on what is being built, the developers are only limited by their own imagination. We are trying to build an open, enthusiastic and equal atmosphere where everyone can create their own project and are welcomed to contribute to other projects.

Popular Public Projects

Though the development of our community is still in its early stage, Miracle Factory has already incubated some exciting public projects. Project Submarine is a computer motherboard defect detection and analysis system. Powered by YOLOv5, it is a fast-responding and powerful defect detection system that allows manufacturers to test their products and analyse the results in real-time. Combined with other machine learning algorithms, it will also be able to forecast critical defects way before they occur.
